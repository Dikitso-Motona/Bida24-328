Annual Youth Empowerment Conference 2025

Date: June 15, 2025   |   Location: Gaborone International Convention Centre

About This Event

Join us for a day of inspiration, education, and networking at the Annual Youth Empowerment Conference. This event features keynote speakers, breakout workshops, and interactive panels focused on leadership, innovation, and community development.

Whether you are a student, young professional, or community leader, this conference is designed to empower you with the skills and connections to make a difference.

Event Schedule

  • 9:00 AM: Registration & Welcome Breakfast
  • 10:00 AM: Opening Keynote Address
  • 11:30 AM: Workshop Sessions
  • 1:00 PM: Networking Lunch
  • 2:30 PM: Panel Discussion
  • 4:00 PM: Closing Remarks & Awards
