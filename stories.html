Survivor Stories

Real Voices , Real Courage. Hear how Speak Up Botswana has helped survivors find safety and healing

"SpeakUp Botswana gave me my life back." – Lerato

Lerato, 29, Gaborone October 2024

For years, I lived in fear, trapped in a cycle of domestic abuse. I felt isolated and believed it was my fault. One day, a friend shared information about SpeakUp Botswana. Hesitantly, I reached out to their hotline. The person on the other end listened without judgment and offered immediate support. They helped me understand my rights and connected me with a safe shelter outside of Gaborone. Leaving was the hardest decision, but SpeakUp Botswana was with me every step of the way. They provided counseling that helped me heal from the trauma and regain my self-worth. Today, I am a survivor, not a victim, and I am building a new life for myself and my children, thanks to the unwavering support of SpeakUp Botswana.

"Breaking the silence was the first step to healing." – Thabo

Thabo, 35, Francistown November 2024

As a male survivor of sexual violence, I felt immense shame and didn't know where to turn. Societal stigma kept me silent for years, leading to anxiety and depression. Searching online one night, I found SpeakUp Botswana's website. Reading about their inclusive approach gave me a glimmer of hope. I contacted their support services and was connected with a counselor who specialized in trauma. It was the first time I felt truly heard and understood. SpeakUp Botswana provided a safe and confidential space for me to process my experiences and begin my healing journey. They also connected me with a support group for male survivors, where I found strength in shared experiences. SpeakUp Botswana didn't just offer help; they offered a lifeline and empowered me to break the silence and reclaim my life.

"I found my voice and my freedom." – Neo

Neo, 41, Maun December 2024

The emotional abuse was insidious, slowly eroding my confidence and sense of self. I was constantly belittled and controlled, making me doubt my own judgment. A friend who had previously sought help from SpeakUp Botswana recognized the signs and encouraged me to reach out. I was hesitant, but the persistent emotional pain pushed me. SpeakUp Botswana’s support team helped me recognize the abuse for what it was and develop a safety plan. They offered resources on setting boundaries and rebuilding my self-esteem. Through their guidance, I found the strength to end the toxic relationship and start the difficult but liberating process of healing. SpeakUp Botswana gave me the tools and the courage to prioritize my well-being and build a future free from manipulation.

"SpeakUp Botswana helped me heal from the shadows of my past." – Palesa

Palesa, 25, Selebi-Phikwe January 2025

The trauma of childhood abuse cast a long shadow over my life, impacting my relationships and my mental health. It felt like a burden I would carry forever. Seeking help was incredibly daunting, but I knew I needed to heal. I learned about SpeakUp Botswana through a community outreach program. Their compassionate and understanding approach made me feel safe enough to share my story. They provided specialized therapy and support tailored to survivors of child abuse. SpeakUp Botswana helped me process the past, understand its impact, and develop coping mechanisms. It was a long and challenging journey, but I never felt alone. Thanks to SpeakUp Botswana, I am learning to live fully in the present and build a future filled with hope and resilience. They gave me the support I needed to reclaim my voice and my life.
